Boxing Ladies

The Boxing Ladies class was created to give more space to women in a sport where the prejudice of it being a men's-only sport still exists.

Often the first contact with boxing is missing, and that is exactly what we want to change. Complementing the mixed classes (men and women), we also offer a space and a specific time exclusively for women. The class is open to all levels and focuses on technique and physical conditioning applied to Boxing.

Project

More Women
in Boxing

Founded in April 2025

The More Women in Boxing project was born in April 2025, from a course promoted by the Portuguese Olympic Committee (COP) focused on equality in sport. Its goal is to bring boxing closer to women and girls, creating opportunities for them to try the sport for the first time. To achieve this, we organise training sessions and special events across the country, for both beginners and those with experience. At the same time, we aim to unite the entire boxing community in Portugal — clubs, athletes, coaches, referees and enthusiasts — promoting sharing, learning and collaboration. 👊🏻
